## Forex FAQs – Your Questions Answered

### What is Forex?

Forex, or foreign exchange trading, is the process of exchanging different currencies on a decentralized global market. As one of the largest and most liquid markets, forex involves buying and selling currencies based on exchange rate fluctuations.

### How Do I Trade Forex?

Forex trading allows you to speculate on whether the price of one currency will rise or fall against another. For example, if you believe the value of the Euro will rise against the US Dollar, you would trade EUR/USD.

### What Are Some Forex Trading Examples?

To better understand forex trading, look at our real-life examples of buying and selling positions on currency pairs.

### What is Margin in Forex?

Margin is essentially a deposit required to open a leveraged trading position. It lets you control more prominent positions with a smaller deposit, multiplying potential gains or losses.
